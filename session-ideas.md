Based on current trends and your technical focus, here are session ideas organized by category:

## Core Systems & Infrastructure

**Linux & Systems Programming:**
- **Kernel Development 101**: Writing your first kernel module
- **Memory Management Deep Dive**: From malloc to slab allocators
- **Linux Performance Tools**: perf, ftrace, and beyond eBPF
- **Systems Programming in Rust**: Building low-level tools safely
- **Container Internals**: What Docker really does under the hood

**Distributed Systems:**
- **Consensus Algorithms Explained**: Raft, Paxos in practice
- **Building Fault-Tolerant Systems**: Patterns and anti-patterns
- **Distributed Tracing with OpenTelemetry**
- **Service Mesh Deep Dive**: Istio, Linkerd internals
- **Event-Driven Architecture**: Kafka, NATS, and beyond

## Emerging Tech & AI

**LLMs & AI (Based on AI and ML being transformative forces in 2025):**
- **Fine-Tuning LLMs on Your Data**: Practical guide
- **RAG Systems from Scratch**: Building context-aware AI apps
- **Prompt Engineering Masterclass**: Beyond basic prompts
- **Running LLMs Locally**: Ollama, llama.cpp, optimization
- **AI Agents**: Building autonomous systems with LangChain/AutoGPT
- **Vector Databases**: Choosing between Pinecone, Weaviate, Milvus

**Modern Development Trends:**
- **Low-Code Tools for Developers**: When and why to use them (relevant as 70% of new business applications will be built using low-code or no-code by 2025)
- **AI-Assisted Coding**: GitHub Copilot, Cursor, beyond autocomplete
- **WebAssembly Deep Dive**: Running near-native code in browsers
- **Edge Computing**: Cloudflare Workers, Fastly Compute@Edge

## Programming Languages

**Language-Specific:**
- **Go Concurrency Patterns**: Goroutines, channels, select
- **Rust Async Programming**: Tokio, async-std explained
- **Zig: The Modern C Alternative**
- **Python Performance Optimization**: From slow to fast
- **TypeScript Advanced Types**: Generics, conditional types
- **Functional Programming in JavaScript**: Immutability, composition

## Cloud Native & DevOps

**Kubernetes & Cloud:**
- **Kubernetes Networking Explained**: CNI, service mesh, ingress
- **GitOps with ArgoCD**: Declarative infrastructure
- **Cloud Cost Optimization**: Practical strategies
- **Serverless Architectures**: When to go serverless, when not to
- **Multi-Cloud Strategies**: Real-world implementations

**DevOps/DevSecOps (Growing trend as DevSecOps is extending to embedded and IoT development):**
- **CI/CD Pipeline Optimization**: Speed up your builds
- **Infrastructure as Code**: Terraform vs Pulumi vs CDK
- **Observability 101**: Metrics, logs, traces
- **Security in CI/CD**: Shifting left effectively

## Databases & Data
